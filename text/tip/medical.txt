It is generally cheaper to make (or find) your own medication than it is to buy it.
If you are planning on fixing up yourself or fixing others, you should be bringing medication to fix every damage type.
Health Analyzers are very useful for diagnosing general medical problems without having to look at someone.
For most medications, injecting the dosage has a higher potency and a faster metabolism rate than medications taken orally.
You can remove defibrillator paddles by clicking on them while the defibrillator unit is on your back, or by alt-clicking it while on the ground.
