## Key Combinations - Combat Hold Block(C) AND Any Mouse Click, Empty Hand: Dodge towards the target (3 second cooldown). Hold Block(C) within 1 second of attack: Parry incoming damage (3 second cooldown). Double Tap Block(C) AND Movement: Dodge in that direction (3 second cooldown). Grab(Ctrl) AND Mouse1 (on Object in World): Grab with Right Hand Grab(Ctrl) AND Mouse2 (on Object in World): Grab with Left Hand
